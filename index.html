About

Prayer times without the clutter

Adantimer is built for one clear job: helping you reach today's prayer times quickly. Instead of burying the schedule behind menus, the page shows your city, the next salah, and the full daily timetable right away.

If you travel often or want to check prayer times for family in another place, you can search a city manually and share the city page directly. That makes Adantimer more useful for long-tail searches such as prayer times in Berlin, prayer times in London, or fajr time in Dubai today.

Search engines tend to reward pages that are helpful, clear, and focused. This page is now structured around real user intent: finding accurate salah times for a specific place and understanding when the next prayer begins.
